Integration Disorder

Schizophrenia is a mind disease. A sick mind we say in the bizz. Contraditorily holding incompatible thoughts in the head. At times thoughts can even frighten. I've lived with this disease my entire adulthood. (What is an adult, I query) Herein lies some resources and tidbits I've picked up. This web page is intended as a portfolio to help me learn job skills. So click, scroll, enjoy.

Caveat

At the top of many lists is the topic of safety. People with schizophrenia often have crises. Not to worry. Make a crisis plan. Thoughts get scary and one may become paranoid. Learn to calm oneself. You are valuable, important and worthwhile so care for yourself. This was quite difficult for me to learn. If you need immediate help, here is a link to 988lifeline which is a website and phone line for people in distress.
