Reasons For Hope
  • Neuroplasticity: the brain is malleable. One's brain's current state which I think is the definition of the mind (I've heard people call this a connectome) changes as one changes one's own thought. Schizophrenia is when the brain state is in an un-self-regulating arrangement. "I'm all over the place," a young lady once told me.
  • Much research and information is free
  • You have to work if able. And many people choose to work helping others. Resources in the community are abundant. Don't give up!
  • Where there is a will there is a way
  • Hope feels good. As does gratitude
Caveat

Beware! This information is from my own experience and is what I have found to help. Every illness is different. Schizophrenia is a grave illness. I have had issues with self harm though I lacked the insight to realize I needed to be aware of this. I remembered the episodes, I just did not process much on them to be aware of their magnitude. Be careful when you use this information and please care for yourself. You are worthwhile and worth the effort to take good care of yourself.
